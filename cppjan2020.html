We're hiring a C++ developer

Columno on The Hub.

Do you have passion for writing C++ code and enjoy making it run as fast as possible? Do you like freedom when choosing development tools and technologies? Do you want to work at a deep tech startup?
We are looking for a full time C++ developer who will help us accelerate our public beta launch date. After the launch of the beta we aim at launching a production release later in 2020. You will be helping us getting there.

We are building state-of-the-art database technology with novel index and query processing strategies. We already have a working prototype. Your job will be to implement the new algorithms and expand on the number of integrations with other modern databases (e.g., Redis, RIAK, Hadoop, ...).
We don’t expect that you are an expert in databases nor is it required to do the job. Currently, the work on the core database technology will be done by others in Columno. The algorithms will be explained and discussed with you before you implement them. But if you have the interest you are more than welcome to join the development of the core algorithms. You will have the possibility to become an expert in modern databases (SQL/NoSQL/NewSQL) at the core level.

We are a very young startup company - founded in Summer 2019. Currently, it is only the founder, Anders Skovsgaard, that is working full time. So, when I write “we” - it’s soon you and me :D We will be staying at a great shared office space so when you get bored of talking with me there will be plenty of others to talk to :)
It is very important to me that you enjoy the daily work and life at the office - in combination with your own life. So, you can get it pretty much as you want.

You have to know C++ very well of course. However, we do not require any formal education or fancy CV if you know your stuff good enough :). Knowledge of databases is a plus but not a requirement.
The position is full time but we focus on what is done instead of time spent.
You are native Danish or English speaker.
The office is located in central Aarhus at the harbour. Free coffee (great espresso/cappuccino).

If you have any questions please write anders@columno.com or call +4571992132.

You can send your application to - you’ve guessed it - anders@columno.com. Please include your resume and a cover letter.
The position start date is Marts 1, 2020 and the application deadline is Jan 31, 2020.
